About Lucinda

I genuinely love
this work.

There's a specific moment I find completely energising — when a founder goes from "I think I know what I want to do" to "I know exactly what to do and in what order." That shift from foggy to focused is what I get out of bed for.

"The vision is almost never the problem. It's the gap between vision and sequenced, executable action — that's where I come in."

I'm a runner and endurance athlete, and I think about founder journeys the same way I think about long races. It's not about going flat out from the start. It's about knowing your route, understanding which sections need effort and which need patience, and having someone alongside you who's studied the terrain. That's what I do for the businesses I work with.

My background is 15+ years building and scaling digital businesses — including the early days of one of the UK's most ambitious e-commerce companies, growing from a small team into a global enterprise. I've made the hard calls, been in rooms where the strategy was wrong, and learned what separates businesses that scale from ones that stall.

What I care about most is that you leave every conversation more capable and more confident than when we started. Not reliant on me — ready to move on your own terms, with a clear sense of what's next.

The Framework

How SCOUT works.

Every engagement — whether a single session or an ongoing partnership — works across five dimensions. Together, they form a complete picture of where your business is and where it needs to go.

C
Commerce
Revenue growth, unit economics, and the commercial levers that actually move the needle.
O

Systems, process, and resilience — so the business works without you in every decision.
U
User Experience
CRO, platform optimisation, and understanding your customer at every touchpoint.
T
Transformation
Change leadership, team capability, and the confidence to lead through complexity.
